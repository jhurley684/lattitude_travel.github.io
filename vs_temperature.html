Does the Latitude Affect The Temperature?


When you make your travel plans are you concerned with how warm or how cold it will be when you get there? For vacation planners it is the most important criteria. People who live in cold climes what to go to warmer climes in the winter. On the other hand, skiers are not interested in going to a ski resort if it's not going to be below 32 degrees fahrenheit. What if they made their plans based on latitude alone? Our weather plot says "yes".

Whether you head north or south, the farther you get away from the equator the colder the temperatures get, with the warmest temperatures grouping near the equator.
